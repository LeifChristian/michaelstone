# Michael Stone

Michael Stone was a poet, a musician, and a friend whose presence left a mark on everyone who knew him.

This space exists so his words, music, and photographs can remain close — not behind a login, not locked in a feed that moves on, but here, quietly, for anyone who wants to remember.

## His music

Michael’s recordings carry the tone of the person he was: honest, searching, and alive. Sit with them. Let them play.

## Photographs

The images gathered here are ordinary and precious at once — moments with friends, days that seemed small at the time, faces we are grateful we still have.

## Remembering

If you are here because you found a sticker, or because someone sent you this link, you are welcome. Stay as long as you like.

> Memory is how we keep company with the people we love.

When you are ready, return to his music, or share this page with someone who should know he is remembered.
